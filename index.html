Bandcast

Bandcast is a Chrome extension that enables you to listen to your Bandcamp music on your TV via Chromecast.

How to use

Bandcast requires Chromecast and the Chrome browser with Google Cast extension.

1. Install Google Cast extension

Open the Chrome browser, and install the Google Cast extension.

2. Install Bandcast

install this Bandcast extension on your Chrome browser.

3. Open your favorite Bandcamp page

Open Bandcamp album page. ex) https://atrocitysolution.bandcamp.com/album/lost-remedies

Yay!! You now see the cast icon on your screen!

4. Enjoy your Bandcast
